SECRET SANTA

Secret Santa is a fun game that helps you create a New Year's mood and make pleasant surprises for your friends, colleagues or relatives. The essence of the game is that each participant secretly chooses one gift recipient from the entire group and buys him something within a pre-agreed budget. Then everyone gets together and exchanges gifts, not knowing who they are from. This way you can get to know each other better, laugh and have fun.
